Header Risk Checker

Is this sketchy?

Paste a raw email header and this page will score how sketchy it looks using common checks like SPF, DKIM, DMARC, domain mismatches, routing oddities, and spam flags. Everything runs in your browser only.

This is a simple heuristic tool, not a forensic verdict. A low score does not guarantee safety, and a high score does not prove malicious intent.

Tip: pasting auto-runs the analysis. You can also press Ctrl+Enter or Cmd+Enter.

How to get full headers

Gmail: open the message, choose More, then Show original.
Outlook desktop: open the message, go to File, then Properties, and copy Internet headers.
Outlook on the web / Microsoft 365: open the message, choose More actions, then View, then View message details.
